##  The Local Cool Down: Social Ice Cream

After a long day of touring homes in the 90-degree heat, you need a win. We recommend heading to Social Ice Cream in the Montecillo area.

* The Vibe: Modern, vibrant, and incredibly refreshing.
* The Recommendation: Try the "Cereal Killer" or one of their seasonal fruit sorbets.
* Live Like a Local Tip: It’s located in a walkable part of the West Side. Grab a cone and stroll through the neighborhood to get a feel for the local architecture and community vibe.

## 📅 June Event Spotlight: El Fresco & Sun City Pride

El Paso truly comes alive in June. If you want to see the city’s heart, check out these two staples:

1. El Fresco Music Series (June 19): Head to Downtown El Paso (1 Civic Center Plaza) for this free outdoor concert. It’s "Noche de Cumbias," so bring your dancing shoes!
2. Sun City Pride: June is Pride month, and El Paso hosts one of the largest celebrations in the Southwest. The energy downtown is unmatched, featuring parades, local vendors, and block parties that show off our city's inclusive spirit.

## 🏠 The Fort Bliss Market Watch: June 2026

We’re seeing a very specific trend in the El Paso real estate market this summer. Unlike the national trend where inventory is growing, El Paso’s active listings actually dropped by over 3% recently.

What this means for you:

* The Price Gap: Our median list price is sitting around $303,000. While that is a 3.2% increase from last year, it is still nearly 28% more affordable than the national average. Your BAH still goes further here than almost anywhere else.
* The Neighborhood "Shortlist": If you want a quick commute to Biggs Army Airfield, look closely at Castner Heights and Mountain View. These areas offer established homes and "bike-friendly" paths that make morning PT commutes a breeze.
* The Seller’s Edge: Because inventory is tight, homes that are priced correctly are moving in under 60 days. If you are selling a home to move to your next duty station, now is the time to list to capture the incoming PCS wave.

> ### 💡 PCS Pro-Tip: The "Utility" Hack
>
> In El Paso, June is when the AC starts working overtime. When you’re under contract on a home, ask your agent to request the last three months of utility bills. It’s the best way to budget for your "Cooling Costs" before you move in.
