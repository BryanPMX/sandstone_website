## The Morning Routine: 2Ten Coffee Roasters

Every great El Paso day starts with the right fuel. If you’re house hunting on the West Side or near the East Side, you’ll likely find our team at 2Ten Coffee Roasters.

* The Vibe: Sleek, industrial, and deeply local.
* The Recommendation: Their "Customs" blend is a must-try.
* Pro Tip: If you’re a military family visiting for a house-hunting trip, their patios are dog-friendly, making it a great spot to review El Paso real estate listings with your four-legged family members.

## May Events: Experience the Sun City Culture

You can’t truly understand living in El Paso until you’ve spent an evening in the mountains. This month marks the return of a local favorite: Cool Canyon Nights.

Held every Thursday at the McKelligon Canyon Amphitheatre, this free concert series is the gold standard for local entertainment. It’s a perfect opportunity for those undergoing a military relocation to meet their new neighbors and see why we’re so proud of our "Sun City" culture. Pack a blanket, grab some local food truck tacos, and enjoy the sunset over the Franklins.

## The Fort Bliss Market Minute: May 2026 Update

If you are currently browsing homes for sale near Fort Bliss, the spring market is in full swing. Here is the data-driven "local" perspective you won't find on the national search portals:

1. BAH Strategy: With the 2026 Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH) adjustments, we are seeing a high volume of E-5 to O-4 personnel choosing to purchase in Northeast El Paso and Horizon City. These areas currently offer the best "square-footage-to-commute" ratio for the main gates.
2. VA Loan Competitiveness: Many sellers in the El Paso area are veterans themselves. This creates a unique environment where VA offers are highly respected and often prioritized, especially when backed by a local military relocation specialist who knows how to navigate the appraisal process.
3. Inventory Alert: New construction is booming near the East Side and Mission Ridge. If you can’t find the perfect "re-sale" home, we have access to builder incentives that can help buy down your interest rate.
